## 💡 TIPS FOR USERS

### Valid Answers:
- **Numbers**: `1`, `2`, `3` (for options)
- **Text**: For name, brand, etc.
- **Multiple choice**: `1,2,3` (comma-separated)
- **Skip**: Type `skip` for optional questions
- **Restart**: Type `restart` to start over

### Invalid Answers:
- ❌ Letters for number questions: `one` instead of `1`
- ❌ Out of range: `9` when only options 1-6 exist
- ❌ Random text for structured questions

### Conversation Features:
- ✅ Bot remembers your answers (session storage)
- ✅ Can restart anytime (type `restart`)
- ✅ Questions adapt based on vehicle type (EV vs Petrol)
- ✅ Multilingual support (EN/HI)
- ✅ QR code automatically generated and sent
